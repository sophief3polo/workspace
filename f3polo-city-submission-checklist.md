# F3 Polo city-by-city free listing checklist

## Before you submit anywhere
- Confirm final event name for each city
- Confirm date, venue, suburb and start/finish times
- Confirm ticket link
- Confirm website link
- Prepare 1 short description and 1 medium description
- Prepare 1 hero image and 1 square image
- Prepare organiser contact email and phone
- Check each site's image size and category rules

## Brisbane
- Submit to Brisbane City Council What's On
- Submit to Eventfinda Australia
- Submit to What's On In Australia
- Submit to Events Box
- Check whether Brisbane City Council requires a business/event category form rather than the general form
- Record submission date and any approval email in tracker

## Sydney
- Submit to City of Sydney What's On
- Submit to What's On In NSW
- Submit to Events in Sydney
- Submit to Eventfinda Australia
- Submit to What's On In Australia
- Submit to Events Box
- Record any moderation/approval notes in tracker

## Melbourne
- Submit to What's On In Melbourne
- Submit to City of Melbourne What's On Listings
- Submit to Eventfinda Australia
- Submit to What's On In Australia
- Submit to Events Box
- Record any image or category requirements in tracker

## After submission
- Save confirmation emails
- Follow up any pending approvals after 3-5 business days
- Check each live listing for branding, dates and links
- Fix any weak copy, formatting or image issues immediately
- Add live listing URLs back into the tracker

## Best order to do this
1. Fill in final event details once
2. Submit national sites first
3. Submit city-specific official sites second
4. Check approval status 3-5 business days later
5. Reuse approved copy across every remaining site
