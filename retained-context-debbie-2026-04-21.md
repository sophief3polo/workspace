## Commercial model and priorities

- Sponsorship is the best-margin revenue line and the main underwriting mechanism for events.
- Sponsorship categories include major sponsor, team sponsors, and alcohol categories like champagne, beer, RTD, wine, and non-alcoholic.
- Private marquees are the highest-value hospitality product, then VIP, then GA.
- VIP margin is around 60 percent.
- GA is not very profitable below about 500 attendees, but margins improve significantly from around 800 to 2,500.
- The company carries head office costs for three full-time staff plus event delivery costs.
- Simon’s top priorities are revenue, sponsorship, and venues.
- The biggest current strategic risk is not landing a major sponsor in each country, especially Australia.
- Other major risks are ticket sales and weather.
- The biggest current opportunity is four strong events in Australia, then six venues in the USA.
- Success means cash flow, profits, sold-out events, and strong sponsorship.
- Failure means running events without sponsorship underwriting them.
