# Hourly ticketing and sponsorship upkeep

- Ticketing updates must be as close to instant as possible, not hourly.
- Keep Flicket ticket sales and Mission Control ticketing summaries updated immediately when changes are detected.
- Every hour: update Sponsorship - Teams & Umpire if sponsor statuses changed.
- Every hour: update Sponsorship - Pourage if sponsor statuses changed.
- Every hour: update Sponsorship - Others if sponsor statuses changed.
- Keep hold/sold formatting accurate as tickets move.
